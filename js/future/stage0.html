Positive Pregnancy Test

Some normal signs of early pregnancy

  • No menstrual cycle
  • May feel sick to your stomach
  • May vomit or just feel queasy
  • Increased urination
  • Breasts may be bigger and/or more tender
  • Increased appetite
  • Increased moodiness

  • Steps you should take now:

  • Start taking prenatal vitamins
  • If you're smoking (or using drugs or alcohol), stop.
  • Try to get lots of sleep.
  • Identify a doctor or nurse-midwife for prenatal care:
  • Meharry Centering Pregnancy
  • Matthew Walker Clinic
  • Vine Hill Community Clinic
  • Make an appointment to confirm the pregnancy
  • See a provider from the list above, or
  • find a Metro Health Department Clinic near you, or
  • contact the Hope Clinic for Women

  • Use the resources in this app to help with:

  • Talking to your parents or guardians
  • Telling your partner about the test results
  • Discussing your options with your family or a counselor
  • Identifying a birth friend -- someone you can confide in
  • Checking your insurance status or eligibility
  • Checking your eligibility for nutritional support
  • Signing up for many different kinds of support services
