Positive Pregnancy Test

  • Some normal signs of early pregnancy

  • No menstrual cycle
  • May be sick to yoursstomach
  • Maybe vomiting
  • Increased urination
  • Breasts may be bigger and/or more tender
  • Increased appetite
  • Increased moodiness
  • Start taking prenatal vitamins
  • If you're smoking (or using drugs or alcohol), stop.
  • Check your insurance status or eligibility.
  • Identify a doctor or nurse-midwife for prenatal care:
  • Meharry Centering Pregnancy
  • Matthew Walker Clinic
  • Vine Hill Community Clinic
  • Make an appointment to confirm the pregnancy
  • See a provider from the list above, or
  • find a Metro Health Department Clinic near you, or
  • contact the Hope Clinic for Women
  • Try to get lots of sleep.
