Staying in School

The fact that you're having a baby doesn't mean you have to drop out of school. You can continue to attend your regular school up until the baby is born. During your recovery, MNPS offers homebound services to help you keep up with your classes.

Then, when you feel up to it, and your doctor gives the okay, you can go back to your old school or switch to a one of Metro's Academies, which feature alternative schedules for working or parenting students.

You may also qualify for vouchers from the state of Tennessee to help you pay for childcare once your baby is born.
