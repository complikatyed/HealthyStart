Staying in School

The fact that you're having a baby doesn't mean you have to drop out of school. There are resources available to help you keep up with school during your pregnancy. Others, including schools with alternative schedules, and childcare vouchers from the state, are designed to help you get back to school once the baby is born. The list below will help you get started with plans to continue your education.


Academies at Opry Mills, Old Cockrill and Hickory Hollow

These schools are for students ages 17-21, and they offer a high school diploma and flexible hours to accommodate student schedules. Students can attend classes in the morning, afternoon or evening.
