When your pregnancy test is positive...

  • No menstrual cycle
  • Upset stomach, maybe vomiting
  • Increased urination
  • Breasts may be bigger or more tender
  • Increased appetite
  • Increased moodiness

  • Start taking prenatal vitamins
  • If you're smoking, get help to stop.
  • Stop drinking alcohol or using drugs.
  • Try to get lots of sleep.
  • Make an appointment to confirm the pregnancy and choose a doctor or nurse-midwife for monthly visits.
