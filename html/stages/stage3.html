Third Trimester

months 7 to 9

  • Most women find the third trimester physically uncomfortable. You'll probably experience several of the following:
    • Discomfort from baby stretching and kicking
    • Bleeding gums
    • Swelling of the ankles and feet
    • Decreased ability to sleep well
    • Increased clumsiness
    • Leg cramps, back pain, aches in your pelvis
    • Practice contractions
    • (also called "Braxton Hicks" contractions)

  • Your prenatal visits will move up to every two weeks, and then once a week in the last month.
  • You may be tested for gestational diabetes.
  • Start tracking your baby's kicks. You should feel at least 10 kicks per hour.
  • Be aware of the signs of preterm labor:

    Get medical help immediately:
    • for bleeding or cramping (like a period, or worse),
    • chest pain or difficulty breathing,
    • fainting, blurred vision, or severe headache;
    • if your baby kicks fewer than 10 times in an hour;
    • if your water breaks or you have vaginal bleeding.

  • You may have difficulty sitting for a long time.
  • Sign up for homebound studies.
    • There are forms for you and your school to complete:
    • http://mnpshomeboundforms.wikispaces.com/
  • Talk with your school counselor about your plans for returning to school after the baby is born.
    • LINKS TO ALTERNATIVE SCHOOLS
    • LINKS TO ALTERNATIVE SCHOOLS
  • If you are planning to breastfeed, you'll need:
    • a place at school to pump your milk
    • refrigerated storage for the pumped milk
  • Make arrangements for childcare now if you can.
    • Vouchers are available from the state
    • You can apply for them after the baby is born.
    • LINK TO VOUCHER SOURCE GOES HERE!

  • Talk with your doctor or midwife about:
    • what birthing classes are available
    • creating a birth plan
    • family planning options after the baby is born
  • Identify a pediatrican for the baby
  • Pack your bag for the hospital
  • You'll need a rear-facing infant carseat.
  • Your baby needs to sleep safely in one of these:
    • crib
    • bassinette
    • "pack and play"
