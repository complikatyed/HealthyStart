The Vocabulary of Pregnancy & Birth

the terms below are listed in alphabetical order

doula
nonmedical person who assists a woman through pregnancy and childbirth
family planning
long-term birth control planning
midwife
has professional training in the care of pregnant women and delivery of babies
nurse practitioner
a nurse with advanced training and clinical experience
obstetrician
a doctor who specializes pregnancy care and the delivery of babies
pediatrician
a doctor who specializes in the care of babies and children
postpartum
after giving birth (can refer to the time period or a woman's condition)
postpartum depression
severe, long-lasting depression that begins after giving birth
prenatal
time between becoming pregnant and when the baby is born
trimester
one of three 3 month-long segments of pregnancy
voucher
a certificate that can be used as payment
